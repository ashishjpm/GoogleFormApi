Make a single responsive page
Make a single responsive page (is usable on laptops and mobile phones) with an image in the background. On the left half of the page, have a heading and a text (Section 1). This full section should be vertically and horizontally centered (in the respective half section). On the right half, create a simple form (Section 2) with one field as email and a submit button. On clicking the submit button, the email should be sent as a post request to the google form link below. In case of mobile phones, the two sections shall be aligned one below the other for better usability.
